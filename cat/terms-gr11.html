Grade 11 — Study by Term Grade 11

Based on the 2026 CAT Annual Teaching Plan. Grade 11 introduces Databases (Access) and extends Word, Excel and HTML. Mid-year and final exams are 120 and 150 marks respectively.

1
Term 1 — Advanced Word, Spreadsheet & Databases
Assessment: SBA Task 1 — Theory Test + SBA Task 2 — Practical Test (min 50 each)
Practical (Solution Development)
  • Word — templates, sections, columns, drop cap, forms
  • Spreadsheets — conditional formatting, absolute ref, ROUND, SMALL, LARGE
  • Databases — tables, data types, primary key, forms
Theory
  • General concepts, input/output devices, POS/ATM
  • Social (disabilities, wearables, power, health)
2
Term 2 — Networks, IF Functions & Mid-Year
Assessment: SBA Task 3 — Mid-Year Exam (P1 120 + P2 120). PAT Phase 1 completed.
Practical (Solution Development)
  • Spreadsheets — IF, COUNTIF, SUMIF, charts
  • Databases — data types, field properties, queries (AND/OR/NOT)
  • Word — styles (create/edit)
  • HTML — tables, comments, attributes, alignment
Theory
  • Processing, storage, cloud storage (SaaS)
  • Networks (LAN/WLAN, components, security)
  • Social (unauthorised access, AI, big data)
3
Term 3 — Internet, IoT, Reports & Mail Merge
Assessment: SBA Task 4 — Practical Test + SBA Task 5 — Alternative Test. PAT Phase 2 completed.
Practical (Solution Development)
  • Databases — reports (wizard, calculations, headers/footers)
  • Spreadsheets — import/export, sheets, print options, linking
  • Word — Mail Merge, references (TOC, footnotes, captions)
  • HTML — lists, images, links (anchor tags)
Theory
  • Software (cloud apps, accessibility, installation)
  • Internet (VoIP, IoT, 4IR, VR/AR, mobile access)
  • Social (social engineering, phishing, IoT impact)
4
Term 4 — Integration, PAT Phase 3 & Final Exam
Assessment: SBA Task 6 — End-of-Year Exam (P1 150 + P2 150). PAT Phase 3 completed (120 marks).
Practical (Solution Development)
  • Spreadsheets — integration, problem solving, troubleshooting
  • Databases — integration, design for scenario
  • Word — integration with spreadsheet
Theory
  • Communications (managing email)
  • Social (malware, computer misuse, data protection)
GRADE 11 EXAM STRUCTURE

Paper 1 (Practical, 150 marks, 3 hrs): Word Processing (~45), Spreadsheets (~40), Databases (~35), Web/HTML (~15), General/Integration (~15).
Paper 2 (Theory, 150 marks, 3 hrs): Section A short questions; Section B (Systems, Internet/Network, Info Management, Social, Solution Dev); Section C integrated scenarios.
