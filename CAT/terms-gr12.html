Grade 12 — Study by Term Grade 12

Based on the 2026 CAT Annual Teaching Plan. Grade 12 reinforces all Grade 10–11 skills and adds advanced functions. The final external exam covers content from all three grades.

1
Term 1 — Advanced Functions & Reports
Assessment: SBA Task 1 — Theory Test + SBA Task 2 — Practical Test (min 50 each). PAT Phase 1 completed.
Practical (Solution Development)
  • Databases — grouped reports, advanced queries, calculated fields
  • Spreadsheets — nested IF, VLOOKUP, COUNTIFS, SUMIFS, date/time
  • Word — links, bookmarks, cross-ref, track changes
Theory
  • General concepts, hardware, buying decisions
  • Software, file management, system requirements
  • Social (user-centred design, AI in education)
2
Term 2 — Text Functions, WAN & Mid-Year
Assessment: SBA Task 3 — Alternative Task + SBA Task 4 — Mid-Year Exam (P1 + P2). PAT Phase 2 completed.
Practical (Solution Development)
  • Spreadsheets — text functions (LEFT, RIGHT, MID, CONCATENATE)
  • HTML — table formatting, cellpadding/spacing, merging
  • Word — mail merge (multiple sources), linking/embedding
  • Databases — changing report source, scenario design
Theory
  • Networks & WAN, Internet services, ISP
  • Social (computer crimes, malware, safeguards)
3
Term 3 — Advanced Charts, OS & Prelim
Assessment: SBA Task 5 — Prelim Exam (P1 + P2). PAT Phase 3 completed before prelim.
Practical (Solution Development)
  • Spreadsheets — advanced charts, trendlines, stacked graphs
  • Word — documents using styles, advanced layout
  • Databases — create for scenario, integration
  • HTML — good web/page design
Theory
  • OS role, utilities, performance factors
  • Video conferencing, browsers, blogs, GPS
  • Social (social networks, remote access, VPN)
4
Term 4 — Consolidation & Final External Exam
External Exam: Paper 1 (Practical, 150) 25% + Paper 2 (Theory, 150) 25% + PAT 25% + SBA 25%.
Practical (Revision — all topics)
  • Word Processing — full revision
  • Spreadsheets — full revision
  • Databases — full revision
  • HTML — full revision
Theory (Revision — all topics)
  • Hardware & case studies
  • Software & File Management
  • Networks & WAN
  • Internet & E-Communication
  • Social Implications
GRADE 12 FINAL ASSESSMENT (25% each)

Paper 1 (Practical, 150 marks, 3 hrs): Word (~45), Spreadsheets (~40), Databases (~35), Web/HTML (~15), General (~15).
Paper 2 (Theory, 150 marks, 3 hrs): Section A (short Qs), Section B (Systems, Internet/Network, Info Mgmt, Social, Solution Dev), Section C (2 integrated scenarios).
PAT (25%) and SBA (25%) make up the rest. Note: the final exam covers Grade 10–12 content.
